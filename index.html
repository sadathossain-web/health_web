ABOUT RUNNING MAD & VIRTUAL RACES

My Race creates virtual running races to raise money for charity and rewards runners with awesome medals and goody bags. A virtual race is one that can be completed at any location or time, meaning you do not have to physically turn up to a race start line.

1

Find your race

Sign up to a race with a distance that challenges you.

2

Run the race

Run anywhere, with anyone or no one!

3

Submit your evidence

Login and upload your race records for our verification

4

Receive your medal

Your medal will be sent out to you!
